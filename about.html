about

A short page about the person behind the blog.

I'm Ben. This is my personal site. It exists so I can write in public at my own pace, on my own domain, without a feed algorithm in the middle.

what you'll find here

Mostly writing. Short posts when I'm working through a small idea, longer pieces when the idea deserves one. The topics will wander — that's the point of a personal site rather than a themed one.

The best way to reach me is by email. I'll add a link here once I'm ready for one.
